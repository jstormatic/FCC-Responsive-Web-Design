Guangzhou

This webpage provides some information about the city in Southern China, Guangzhou.

General Information

Guangzhou, also known as Canton is the largest city within the province of Guangdong in Southern China. It's the capital of Guangdong and is home to approximately 66 million people.

Guangzhou is classified as one of China's nine National Central Cities. By urban resident population, Guangzhou is ranked as the 5th most populous city in China.

Cantonese

The main language spoken in Guangzhou is Cantonese. However, over the years, there has been a shift towards Mandarin. However, compared with other dialects within China, it's still far from becoming extinct as many residents in Hong Kong and Taiwan still mainly speak Cantonese.
